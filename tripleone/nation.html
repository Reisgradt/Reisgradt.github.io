About
Founded by James Holding, TripleOne is a new concept, a "community" company (the first decentralized company to ever exist), where every one is free to work together, share ideas, increase profits and increase shares worth.
Joining is free but you can only join if someone invites you. When you join, you have lifetime shares (%) in the company. There are multiple ways to gain more shares. Every month, the nation's profits are distributed to all members based on how many shares and points they have. Revenues are generated through multiple channels such as retail, products & services, local & online businesses and more.
Jobs are created from the businesses we open and offered to members of the nation. Being part of the nation gives you access to different perks such as private stores and services around the world. TripleOne is not just a company but an actual nation, a virtual country, or simply a decentralized company.

Points
10% of the nation’s monthly profits is distributed to members with points
Owning shares is not the only way to generate profits for your account. Points are awarded to your account when you participate in certain activities. They can be earned when voting, sharing ideas, etc...an overall participation.
At the end of each month, 10% of the nation's profit is distributed to members based on the amount of points they havegathered. Here's an example: Let's say the nation made $10m that month. $1m is set to be distributed as a bonus. You have 1 point and the rest of the members have 0 points. That whole $1m goes into your account. Distribution is based on the amount of points othermembers and you have. Points are reset every month. Even though points reset every month, they stay forever for your rank. Points are the only way to increase your rank in the nation.

Voting System
The nation's voting system was created to enhance decentralization. Votes can be used on 4 things.
1
Project ideas/Suggestions presented by members.
2
Stock market investment propositions/portfolios.
3
Job application from a member applying for a job at one of our companies.
4
Community forum posts.
There are two types of votes. Normal votes and Power votes. Power votes are explained below.
Votes can be Positive or Negative. When votes are processed, points are awarded to the member/receiver. The calculation for the points is: (Positive Votes - Negative Votes Power) = Points.
You cannot lose points. The minimum is 0. Negative votes have power based on the rank of the member who voted. Positive votes don't. That is, to prevent certain members from giving votes to themselves to acquire points. If the member's rank is 8 for example, the negative voting power would be 16. (Rank * 2) = Negative Voting Power. You can only vote once(1) per post, project, suggestion or portfolio

Power Votes are stackable votes, they don’t reset. Power Votes can be acquired by being an active member in the nation.
Project Suggestions
Members can suggest the nation to start a new project. A detailed plan of the project and budget must be presented, and if accepted by the members through voting, The TripleOne Company and its team must start working on the project. The budget cannot go over 20% of our total balance.
When a project is accepted, jobs are created for members to apply, and again, members decide who gets the job. The member who presented the project gets a 2% profit share of it for life. That project is also included in our assets bringing in more revenues to the nation.
Jobs
A list of jobs offered by the nation for companies we own are listed on the jobs page. Jobs are either local or online depending on the type of business that needs workers. Jobs can only be accepted by the members. Members decide who gets the job.
Users can suggest new jobs to be created. If a suggestion gets enough votes, the job is created and offered to members.
Currency
There are two types of currencies in your account. The US Dollar (USD) and the TripleOne dollar (TOPD) also known as the TripleOne Private Dollar. TripleOne dollars are awarded when you gain points. 1 Point = 0.001 TOPD.
You can send TripleOne dollars to members in the nation by browsing to the wallet page. TOPD can be sent anonymously, with no username attached to the transaction, hence the reason why it is called TripleOne Private Dollar. It can also be sent publicly, with your username attached. Soon, TripleOne dollars can be used to purchase products and services at any of our companies. The nation's profits are sent to you in US Dollars only.
