About
Founded by James Holding, TripleOne is a new concept, a "community" company (the first decentralized company to ever exist), where every one is free to work together, share ideas, increase profits and increase shares worth.
Joining is free but you can only join if someone invites you. When you join, you have lifetime shares (%) in the company. There are multiple ways to gain more shares. Every month, the nation's profits are distributed to all members based on how many shares and points they have. Revenues are generated through multiple channels such as retail, products & services, local & online businesses and more.
Jobs are created from the businesses we open and offered to members of the nation. Being part of the nation gives you access to different perks such as private stores and services around the world. TripleOne is not just a company but an actual nation, a virtual country, or simply a decentralized company.

In our nation, shares are acquired when you join or invite someone. The monthly nation’s profit is distributed as dividend to members who own shares.
